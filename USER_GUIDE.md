# Mechanic Workshop Billing App - User Guide

## Welcome to Your Mechanic Billing App!

This app is designed to be simple and easy to use, even if you have trouble reading or typing. It uses voice input for most of the work, and only requires typing for amounts.

## Getting Started

### Main Screen

When you open the app, you'll see two large buttons:

- **CREATE NEW BILL** - To make a new bill
- **VIEW PREVIOUS BILLS** - To see old bills

The app will speak instructions to help you navigate.

## Creating a New Bill

### Step 1: Select Language

- Tap the language button (üåê) at the top to change between English, Hindi, or Tamil
- The app will speak in your selected language

### Step 2: Customer Details

1. **Customer Name**

   - Tap the microphone button (VOICE) next to "Customer Name"
   - Speak the customer's name clearly
   - The app will write it down for you

2. **Vehicle Number**
   - Tap the microphone button (VOICE) next to "Vehicle Number"
   - Speak the vehicle number clearly (e.g., "MH 12 AB 1234")
   - The app will write it down

### Step 3: Add Work Done

1. Tap the microphone button next to "Describe the work done"
2. Speak what work was done (e.g., "Engine oil change", "Brake repair")
3. Type the amount using the keyboard (e.g., "2000")

To add more work:

- Tap "+ ADD WORK" button
- Repeat the steps above

### Step 4: Add Spare Parts

1. Tap the microphone button next to "Describe the spare part"
2. Speak the spare part name (e.g., "Oil filter", "Brake pads")
3. Type the amount using the keyboard (e.g., "300")

To add more spare parts:

- Tap "+ ADD SPARE PART" button
- Repeat the steps above

### Step 5: Advance Amount (Optional)

- If the customer gave money in advance, type that amount
- If not, leave it blank

### Step 6: Create Bill

- Check the total amount and balance
- Tap "CREATE BILL" button
- The app will save your bill

## Viewing Previous Bills

1. Tap "VIEW PREVIOUS BILLS" on the main screen
2. You'll see a list of all bills
3. Tap any bill to see its details
4. To send the bill on WhatsApp:
   - On the bill details screen, tap "SEND BILL ON WHATSAPP"
   - The bill details will be sent to WhatsApp

## Offline Mode

- If you don't have internet, you can still create bills
- The bills will be saved on your phone
- When internet comes back, bills will sync automatically

## Troubleshooting

### Voice Not Recognized

- Check if microphone permission is allowed
- Speak more clearly
- Try again

### App Not Responding

- Close and reopen the app
- Check your internet connection

### WhatsApp Not Opening

- Make sure WhatsApp is installed
- Check if the app has permission to open other apps
